A Channel Link can look like this:

- watch?v=VIDEO_ID (can have more URL parameters)
- channel/CHANNEL_ID
- CHANNEL_ID (MUST start with 'UC' otherwise assumed to be handle)
- @CHANNEL_HANDLE (the '@' is recommended but not needed)

All of those can be preceded by
"youtube.com/" or
"youtu.be"
